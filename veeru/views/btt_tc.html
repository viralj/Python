Help

a) Login/Signup using your social media account to use BitTacToe
b) Add/Update your BitCoin wallet address
c) Make some satoshi to play the game against online users

Note: Each game requires to have minimum 500 satoshi. The reward service is automated. You will earn and receive your rewards automatically. You can expect to receive your reward in Bitcoin on 1st of every month. Minimum satoshi requirement is 50,000. If you do not have enough satoshi, you will not receive Bitcoin(s) reward. Remaining satoshi of your account will be carried over to next month and you will be able to receive your total reward in next month.
